Prophetic Life Embassy — Church Management System 
Implementation Roadmap (Functional + User Story Clarity Only)

Core Functional Areas (Modules)
- Attendance Management
- First-Time Visitors & Partners
- Expense & Finance Tracking
- Roles & Access Control
- Departments / Ministry Units
- Appointment & Consultation Scheduling
- Membership (Adults & Children)
- Ministry & Volunteer Management

User Stories — Clear and Simple

1. Attendance Management
User Story:
  As a church administrator, I want to record attendance for each church service (Wednesday and Sunday), so I can monitor participation and growth.
Functional Details:
- Record number of people present
- Choose type of service (Wednesday, Sunday, special)
- View attendance by date or period
- Generate reports (weekly, monthly, yearly totals)
- Automatic running totals as entries are added

2. First-Time Visitors & Partner Management
User Story:
  As a PR or Usher, I want to register first-time visitors and categorize them as general visitors or ministry partners, so we can follow up effectively.
Functional Details:
- Capture name, phone, and visit date
- Select visitor category (general or partner)
- Track follow-up progress
- Automatically send monthly SMS to partners

3. Expense Management
User Story:
  As a finance officer, I want to record church expenses, so we can maintain financial accountability and track spending trends.
Functional Details:
- Input expense details (description, amount, category)
- View expenses by category or month
- See monthly spending summaries

4. Roles & Access Control
User Story:
  As a system administrator, I want different users to have different levels of access based on their role in the church, so operations remain organized and secure.
Functional Details:
- Create roles such as Pastor, PR, Usher, Finance, Media, Intercessor
- Decide what each role can view or update
- Restrict sensitive information to authorized users

5. Departments (Ministry Units)
User Story:
  As a church leader, I want to create departments and organize members in them, so ministry oversight becomes easier.
Functional Details:
- Add ministries such as Media, Prayer Team, Welfare, Music
- Assign members to departments
- Identify leaders and active members within each department

6. Appointment & Consultation Scheduling
User Story:
  As a pastor or church office team, I want people to book appointments for counseling, prayer, and special meetings, so we can manage pastoral care effectively.
Functional Details:
- Accept appointment requests
- Approve, reschedule, or cancel appointments
- View upcoming consultation schedules
- Track appointment status (pending, confirmed, completed, cancelled)

7. Membership (Adults & Children)
User Story:
  As a church administrator, I want to maintain a full member record, including children, so we have a complete database of everyone in the church.
Functional Details:
- Store personal details (name, phone, address, date of birth, etc.)
- Record membership status (active, inactive, visitor, partner)
- Register children under parents or guardians
- Track when someone joined the church

8. Ministry & Volunteer Management
User Story:
  As a ministry coordinator, I want to assign volunteers to service roles, so we can plan and organize all church activities smoothly.
Functional Details:
- Create volunteer roles for services
- Assign volunteers for specific church days
- Mark attendance for workers
- Track ministry engagement and participation

Suggested Dashboard Overview (Non-technical)
- Attendance this week vs last week
- Total first-time visitors this month
- Number of active partners
- Total expenses recorded this month
- Number of volunteers assigned for upcoming services
- Next 10 scheduled appointments
